Top Destinations in Kottayam

Illikkal kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a Thalanadu located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India.The distance from kottayam railway station to illikal kallu is 57km. Situated at around 4000 feet above sea level, Illickal Kallu is a major tourist attraction in Thalanadu.

Ilaveezhapoonchira

Ilaveezhapoonchira is a tourist destination located in Melukavu village in Kottayam district near Kanjar. Ilaveezha Poonchira is surrounded by three enchanting hillocks - Mankunnu, Kodayathoormala and Thonippara. This makes the place ideal for trekking.

Experience Kottayam

VILLAGE LIFE

Village Life of Kottayam is a symphony composed on strings of beauty painted with myriad traditions and cultures.

BACKWATERS

The scenic backwaters of Kottayam comprise serene stretches of lakes, canals and lagoons located parallel to the coast of Arabian Sea.

CUISINE

Kottayam is every food-lover's paradise. Both vegetarians and non-vegetarians have a wide variety of dishes to choose from.

ARTFORMS

The Margamkali is a ritual Xian folk dance in which twelve people dance around a traditional oil lamp (Nilavilakku).
